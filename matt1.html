What is MuushroomrGal

MuushroomrGal is a relaxing retro pixel art style game were you play as Shmarilla a special mushroom girl that was born with the power to creat at attatch bioluminsint mushrooms to things. In MuushroomrGal the people at the big city have started to deforest your home, so shmarilla has decided to take matters into her own hands and start creating and atttaching her mushrooms to the people taking down the forest. But she doesnt stop there, she decided to take over the whole city and convert it into a mushroom paradise.
