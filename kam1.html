Throne of Shadows

Throne of Shadows: Legends of Terrasen

Throne of Shadows is an open world RPG that lets you explore Terrasen and Erilea in a world that you help create in the aftermath of the war against the Valg.

Gameplay

Explore Terrasen in a guided open world adventure. Meet all your favorite characters, Aelin and Rowan, Aedion and Lysandra, Elide and Lorcan, and all the rest.
