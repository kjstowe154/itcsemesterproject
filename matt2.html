Monster Rhythm

What is Monster Rhythm?

Monster Rhythm is a game where you play as a young boy named Maestro. Maestro was born with a special talent that allwos him to conduct and tame the musical monsters. One day there was a great sound that reverberated throught the whole contenent, and the local monsters have started to go wild and rebel.but thankfully for the contenent of music land Maestro can save the day.

GamePlay

Monster Rhythm is a top down 2d rpg were you go throught the content to fight monsters and figure out the mitory of the mosters. in order to tame a monster you engage it in a musical battle! You will face off against the monster and a musical staff will be summoned, and then the monster will play a rhythm tha you have to match!
